Solution

Our solution to this heart renching problem is for there to be better procedures to making sure that someone is supposed to be deported. there are times people are wrongly misplaced. To prevent people from being forcefully and wrongfully displaced there needs to be better laws to prevent it. We plan to start in America and move our way across the world. We want to talk to federal government agents to get the problems changed and to have a more sophisticated displacemnt process. We want to supporting the government for better systems of governance for IDPs and returning refugees by addressing rule of law and accountability. also we plan to strengthening national institutions to progressively deliver greater access to basic services for displaced populations and affected communities and supporting the countries in reducing and managing risks of conflict and natural disasters, the major drivers of displacement. We plan to have a connection to the displaced people through our embassies. We will provide house and food if possible and try to connect them to their family.

We plan to accomplish our goal through donations so please fill out the contact form at the end of the pages to donate.
